About Us

Who We Are — Why the Rabbit?

Lama Educational Foundation is a new, British Council-certified educational consultancy based in Boudha, Kathmandu. We were founded with a clear mission: to be Nepal's most trusted, transparent, and expert-led UK study destination specialist.

लामा एजुकेशनल फाउन्डेशन बौद्ध, काठमाडौंमा स्थित एक नयाँ, ब्रिटिश काउन्सिल प्रमाणित शैक्षिक परामर्श केन्द्र हो।

Our logo — the graduating rabbit — represents our core belief: that with the right knowledge, guidance, and speed, every Nepali student can leap toward a world-class UK education. Quick, intelligent, and always moving forward.

Why the United Kingdom

6 Reasons to Study in the UK

The UK offers one of the world's most respected and rewarding study experiences for international students.

01

🏆 World-Class Universities

Home to Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, UCL, and 18 of the world's top 100 universities. A UK degree is globally recognised and respected.

02

⚡ One-Year Master's

Complete a full Master's degree in just 12 months — saving time and money compared to 2-year programmes in other countries.

03

💼 Graduate Route Visa

Stay and work in the UK for 2 years after graduation (3 years for PhD graduates) — gaining international work experience.

04

🌍 Diverse Environment

Over 600,000 international students study in the UK. You'll build a truly global network from day one.

05

💷 Part-Time Work

Work up to 20 hours per week during term time to support living costs — helping you gain UK work experience alongside studies.

06

📚 Quality Education

UK universities are regulated by the QAA. Degrees are research-led, practically focused, and globally valued by employers.

Student Journey

Your Path to the UK — Step by Step

A clear, structured process that takes you from your first question to your first day at a UK university.

1. Free Counseling Session

Meet our advisor in Boudha or online. Discuss your academic background, goals, and budget. Get honest, unbiased advice.

1
2

2. University Selection

Receive a personalised shortlist of UK universities and courses based on your profile, IELTS score, and career goals.

3. Application Submission

We prepare and submit your university applications — UCAS or direct — with all required supporting documents.

3
4

4. Offer Letter Received

Your university issues a conditional or unconditional offer. We guide you on meeting conditions and accepting the right offer.

5. CAS Letter

Once fees are paid and conditions met, the university issues your Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS number).

5
6

6. UK Student Visa Application

We prepare your complete visa application — financial evidence, TB test, biometrics, and full UKVI compliance check.

7. Pre-Departure Briefing

Orientation session covering UK life, NHS, banking, transport, accommodation, and what to expect on arrival.

7
✈

8. Fly to the UK! 🇬🇧

Your journey begins. Collect your BRP, register with your university, and start building your international future.
